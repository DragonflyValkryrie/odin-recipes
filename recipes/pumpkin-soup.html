Pumpkin Soup

Description

Pumpkin soup is a comforting and velvety-smooth dish that celebrates the rich, earthy flavors of fresh pumpkins. This hearty soup is a perfect blend of creamy texture and savory goodness, seasoned with aromatic spices and herbs. It's a delightful autumn classic that warms the soul and embodies the essence of the season in every spoonful. Whether enjoyed as a starter or a wholesome meal, pumpkin soup is a heartwarming and satisfying choice for cooler days.

Ingredients

  • Stock: Use store-bought or homemade chicken stock.
  • Pumpkin puree: Use store-bought or homemade pumpkin puree.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and a clove of garlic lend bold flavor.
  • Seasonings: The pumpkin soup is seasoned with salt and whole black peppercorns.
  • Whipping cream: Whipping cream creates a smooth, luxurious texture.
  • Fresh herbs: Flavor the soup with fresh thyme and use fresh parsley as a garnish.

Steps

  1. Boil the stock, pumpkin, onion, garlic, thyme, salt and pepper.
  2. Reduce the heat and simmer.
  3. Blend in batches, return to the pot, then stir in the cream.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley.
